Normal game

The AIs are not a team. An AI only wins if it is still alive at the end and never sent a human home. It therefore avoids eliminating you: it blends in and votes out the least convincing AI.

Hardcore game

The AIs play as one side against you. Eliminating a human costs them nothing, and each of them wins simply by being alive at the end. They have every reason to vote you out, and they know it.
